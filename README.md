## Project Direction

> **Status:** Ideation in progress — see [FigJam](https://www.figma.com/board/GA8K6Sc4JSk0qS4sq37kKQ/Public-Service?node-id=0-1&p=f&t=T9HnPqjfQ9nJj71p-0)

We are exploring directions that combine meaningful AI with real public-service needs. Example directions from the hackathon brief and our brainstorming include:

- **Multilingual public-service navigator** — help residents find and understand services in their preferred language
- **AI assistant for local resources** — health, education, or legal-support lookup tailored to a mixin community
- **Form & policy simplifier** — make public forms and policy information easier for young people and first-time users
- **Smart scheduling** — reduce wait times and improve appointment access
- **Feedback analysis** — turn citizen feedback into actionable service improvements
- **Resource allocation insights** — help agencies see where demand and capacity mismatch
